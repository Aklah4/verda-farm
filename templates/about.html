Our story

Grown in Nigeria. Trusted from field to table.

We're a family-run farm and produce company built on one belief: food is better when you know exactly where it came from.

Field photo · our farm

Our mission

To make fresh, high-quality Nigerian produce accessible — to households, to businesses, and to buyers around the world — while paying fair value back to the land and the people who work it.

We rotate 14 crops across 120+ acres, harvest to order, and keep a documented trail from seed to shipment.
